our mission

Reduce Child Retraction by half by 2033

400M is a non-profit launched to fund and distribute technological solutions for retracted children's crisis

~400 Million

Children are retracted in one of the following ways:
~163 Million

Orphaned/ Abandoned

Orphaned - unsupervised or partially supervised having lost 1 or both parents, and/or living in an orphanage.

Abandoned - left or sold by custodians to slavery, criminal syndicates, or guerilla militia.
~168 Million

Invisible

Invisible - unregistered in the formal census, effectively severing the child from access to any kind of civil service (healthcare, education, shelter, nutritional safety)

about us

What we want to do

400m is a non-profit organization dedicated to solving the global orphan children crisis. We believe that by investing in these children, we can create a better future for our world.

To make homes for the hundreds of millions of lost orphaned children

To help find the solutions for disabled orphaned children that don't get adopted

To help adults who want and can experience parenthood, but don't follow the traditional trajectories, or can't afford the adoption process, and are looking for a new paradigm

To bring more and more innovation to this crisis in the hope of alleviating the suffering

Learn more: THE CRISIS

Our solution

Targeted technological intervention that can simplify and expedite processes as well as mitigate costs of the adoption process worldwide.

Short term goal

Create a digital ecosystem of Social, Financial and Legislative solutions driven by technology, and develop/deploy the first solutions

Long term goal

Design and fund an incentive architecture of various financial instruments, to continuously encourage and support technological innovation pushing to resolve the crisis.

Our Core Values

We think only in systemic, holistic terms

We seek omni-considerate, scalable solutions. If a solution is uni-faceted and can’t be scaled - resources have been improperly allocated

We promote and support diversity, inclusion, and believe that so should the regulator

We support boots-on-the-ground organizations and people who deliver local aid (non-scalable)

As a non-profit, we believe in striving financial independence by making it everybody’s business to help us, and we will use the generosity bestowed upon us by donors and patrons of our cause to create a self-sustainable paradigm for 400M
